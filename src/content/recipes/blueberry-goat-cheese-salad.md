---
title: Blueberry & Goat Cheese Salad
description: A savory way to use the bucket — greens, fresh blueberries, toasted nuts, and goat cheese with a warm blueberry-balsamic dressing.
category: savory
prepTime: 15 min
cookTime: 5 min
totalTime: 20 min
servings: 4 as a side
difficulty: Easy
order: 6
ingredients:
  - 6 cups mixed salad greens or baby arugula
  - 1 cup fresh blueberries, divided
  - ½ cup crumbled goat cheese
  - ⅓ cup toasted pecans or walnuts
  - ¼ small red onion, thinly sliced
  - 3 tablespoons olive oil
  - 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  - 1 teaspoon honey or maple syrup
  - Salt and pepper
instructions:
  - Make the dressing- in a small pan, warm the olive oil, balsamic, honey, and ½ cup of the blueberries over medium heat. Cook 3–4 minutes, mashing the berries, until slightly syrupy. Season with salt and pepper and let cool a few minutes.
  - Pile the greens on a platter. Scatter over the remaining blueberries, goat cheese, toasted nuts, and red onion.
  - Drizzle the warm dressing over the top and serve right away.
notes:
  - Add grilled chicken or a handful of cooked farro to make it a meal.
  - The warm dressing softens the greens slightly — dress just before serving.
---
