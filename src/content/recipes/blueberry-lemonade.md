---
title: Fresh Blueberry Lemonade
description: Cloudy-pink, tart-sweet lemonade with a blueberry syrup stirred through — the drink for a hot afternoon in the rows.
category: drinks
prepTime: 15 min
cookTime: 10 min
totalTime: 25 min
servings: About 6 glasses
difficulty: Easy
order: 4
ingredients:
  - 2 cups fresh blueberries
  - 1 cup sugar
  - 1 cup water (for the syrup)
  - 1 cup fresh lemon juice (about 5–6 lemons)
  - 4 cups cold water (to finish)
  - Ice, lemon slices, and mint, to serve
instructions:
  - Combine the blueberries, sugar, and 1 cup water in a small saucepan. Bring to a simmer and cook 8–10 minutes, mashing the berries, until syrupy.
  - Strain the syrup through a fine sieve, pressing on the solids. Let it cool.
  - In a large pitcher, combine the blueberry syrup, lemon juice, and 4 cups cold water. Stir well and taste — add water or a little more sugar syrup to balance.
  - Serve over plenty of ice with lemon slices and mint.
notes:
  - Save the strained blueberry pulp to swirl into yogurt or oatmeal.
  - For a grown-up version, top with sparkling wine instead of some of the water.
