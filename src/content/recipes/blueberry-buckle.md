---
title: Blueberry Buckle
description: A tender cake packed with berries under a cinnamon-crumb top — halfway between coffee cake and dessert, and welcome any time of day.
category: dessert
prepTime: 20 min
cookTime: 45 min
totalTime: 1 hr 5 min
servings: One 9-inch cake
difficulty: Easy
featured: true
order: 7
ingredients:
  - 2 cups all-purpose flour
  - 2 teaspoons baking powder
  - ½ teaspoon salt
  - ½ cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, softened
  - ¾ cup sugar
  - 1 egg
  - ½ cup milk
  - 1 teaspoon vanilla
  - 2½ cups fresh blueberries
  - "For the crumb- ½ cup sugar, ⅓ cup flour, ½ teaspoon cinnamon, ¼ cup cold butter"
instructions:
  - Heat the oven to 375°F. Butter a 9-inch square or round pan.
  - Whisk the flour, baking powder, and salt together.
  - Beat the butter and sugar until fluffy, then beat in the egg, milk, and vanilla. Stir in the flour mixture just until combined.
  - Fold in the blueberries and scrape the batter into the pan.
  - Make the crumb- rub together the sugar, flour, cinnamon, and cold butter with your fingers until crumbly. Scatter over the batter.
  - Bake 40–50 minutes, until a pick in the center comes out clean. Cool at least 20 minutes before cutting.
notes:
  - Wonderful slightly warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  - Keeps, covered, at room temperature for two days.
