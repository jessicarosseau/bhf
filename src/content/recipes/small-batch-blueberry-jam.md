---
title: Small-Batch Blueberry Jam
description: A quick refrigerator jam, no special equipment — a jar of summer to keep in the fridge or freezer.
category: preserves
prepTime: 10 min
cookTime: 25 min
totalTime: 35 min
servings: About 2 cups
difficulty: Easy
order: 5
ingredients:
  - 4 cups fresh blueberries
  - 1 cup sugar
  - 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  - 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  - Pinch of salt
instructions:
  - Combine everything in a wide, heavy saucepan. Let it sit 10 minutes so the berries begin to release their juice.
  - Bring to a boil over medium-high, stirring often and mashing some of the berries against the side of the pan.
  - Reduce to a lively simmer and cook 15–20 minutes, stirring frequently, until thick and glossy and a spoonful sets on a chilled plate.
  - Cool, then transfer to clean jars. Keep in the fridge up to 3 weeks, or freeze up to 3 months.
notes:
  - This is a soft-set refrigerator jam, not shelf-stable canned preserves — keep it cold.
  - For a smoother jam, mash more of the berries as they cook; for a chunky one, mash less.
---

Not shelf-stable canning — just an easy small batch to enjoy now and share.
