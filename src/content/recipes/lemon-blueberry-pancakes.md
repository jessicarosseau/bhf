---
title: Lemon–Blueberry Pancakes
description: Fluffy buttermilk pancakes folded with fresh berries and a little lemon — a proper summer-morning breakfast.
category: breakfast
prepTime: 10 min
cookTime: 15 min
totalTime: 25 min
servings: About 12 pancakes
difficulty: Easy
order: 3
ingredients:
  - 2 cups all-purpose flour
  - 2 tablespoons sugar
  - 2 teaspoons baking powder
  - ½ teaspoon baking soda
  - ½ teaspoon salt
  - 2 cups buttermilk
  - 2 large eggs
  - 3 tablespoons melted butter, plus more for the pan
  - Zest of 1 lemon
  - 1½ cups fresh blueberries
instructions:
  - Whisk the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
  - In another bowl, whisk the buttermilk, eggs, melted butter, and lemon zest.
  - Pour the wet into the dry and stir just until combined — lumps are good. Let the batter rest 5 minutes.
  - Heat a griddle or skillet over medium and brush with butter. Pour ¼-cup rounds and scatter a few blueberries onto each.
  - Cook until bubbles form and the edges look set, about 2 minutes, then flip and cook 1–2 minutes more.
  - Keep warm in a low oven while you finish the batch. Serve with maple syrup and extra berries.
notes:
  - No buttermilk? Stir 2 tablespoons lemon juice into 2 cups milk and let it sit 5 minutes.
  - Scattering the berries onto the poured batter (instead of stirring them in) keeps them from breaking.
---
